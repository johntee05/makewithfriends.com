Brand Overview

Make With Friends is a cozy, collaborative indie game studio based in Malaysia. We craft heartfelt experiences through games, storytelling, and community-driven projects. Our name isn’t just a label — it’s our manifesto. We believe in making with friends, not just for them.

Our studio atmosphere is playful yet professional — like a fun creative workshop where everyone’s ideas matter. We’re the team that makes magic with a sketchbook, a good cup of teh-tarik, and honest teamwork

Mission

To craft heartfelt, collaborative games and experiences that feel cozy, personal, and joyfully handmade — and to do it with the same kindness and curiosity we’d bring to a creative afternoon with friends.

Vision

To be a leading voice in Southeast Asian indie games — known for emotionally resonant storytelling, thoughtful design, and a studio culture that celebrates collaboration, creativity, and community.
